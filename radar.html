Radar

An operator’s map of what is becoming real.

obney.ai/radar should not be a toy trend board. It should be the field guide for leaders deciding what to adopt, trial, assess, or hold as AI becomes part of organizational work.

Adopt

Specs as source of truth

Human intent becomes durable acceptance criteria before agents touch production code.

Trial

Human approval workflows

Approvals, review gates, and operator handoffs become product primitives.

Assess

Persistent agent workspaces

Powerful when isolated, logged, reversible, and scoped. Dangerous when unbounded.

Hold

Generic AI training

Tool demos decay. Workflow ownership, judgment, and systems thinking compound.
